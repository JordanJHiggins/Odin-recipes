Shrimp creole

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 Tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion , diced
  • 1 rib celery , diced
  • 1 green bell pepper , diced
  • 2 cloves garlic , minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/3 cup dry white wine
  • 1 15oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Hot pepper sauce, to taste
  • 2 lb uncooked shrimp

Preparation

  1. Saute veggies - Add a generous amount of butter to a large sauce pan over medium heat. Toss in onion, celery, bell pepper, and garlic. Saute until veggies are soft.
  2. Add flour and spices - In a mixing bowl, combine thyme,salt,pepper,cayenne and flour. Stir into veggies and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Deglaze the pan - with white wine, then add chicken stock, tomatoes, bay leaves and hot sauce. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, stir occasionally.
  4. Add shrimp - add uncooked, peeled shrimp and cook for a few minutes, until shrimp are firm and pink throughout.
